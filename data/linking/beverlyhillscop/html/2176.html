Ronny Cox Born Daniel Ronald Cox July 23, 1938 (age 86) Cloudcroft, New Mexico Years Active 1972-present Occupation Actor, author, musician Character Andrew Bogomil Film(s) Beverly Hills Cop Beverly Hills Cop II Ronny Cox is an American character actor, author, and singer-songwriter. He portrayed Lieutenant Andrew Bogomil in Beverly Hills Cop and Beverly Hills Cop II . Overview [ ] Ronny Cox made his acting debut in the 1972 film, Deliverance and published a book in 2012 that featured his experiences on the set. He starred in the short-lived CBS series Apple's Way from 1974-1975 and went on to be featured in a handful of television series. In 1987, he was in RoboCop , which is considered a cult-classic. In 1984, he appeared in Beverly Hills Cop as Lt. Andrew Bogomil , a role he reprised in the sequel . Cox is also an accomplished musician who has been inducted in the New Mexico Music Hall of Fame and performs 100s of shows per year.
