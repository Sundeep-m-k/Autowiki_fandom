Overview [ ] Martin Brest is a critically and commercially acclaimed director who made his biggest films in the 70s, 80s, 90s, and early 2000s. He was recruited by Don Simpson and Jerry Bruckheimer to direct Beverly Hills Cop , a project that underwent several major changes before its release in 1984. The film was a smash success and garnered two Golden Globe nominations and an Academy Award nomination. Brest went on to cast Tom Cruise and Dustin Hoffman in Rain Man (1988), another cult classic film. Other major films directed by Brest include Midnight Run (1988) which earned him a Golden Globe nomination for Best Motion Picture, and Scent of a Woman (1992) which earned him a Golden Globe award and four Academy Awards.
