The Star Firestar M-45 is a Spanish single-action pistol chambered for the .45ACP round. It is modeled after the American Colt M1911A1 pistol . Its weight is two and a half pounds with a fully loaded magazine of 7 rounds and one extra in the chamber. The trigger pull is 4.5 to 5 pounds. It was seen in Beverly Hills Cop III .
