Eddie Murphy Born Edward Regan Murphy April 3, 1961 New York City, USA Years Active 1976-present Occupation Actor, comedian, writer, producer Character Axel Foley Film(s) Beverly Hills Cop Beverly Hills Cop II Beverly Hills Cop III Beverly Hills Cop: Axel F Eddie Murphy is an American comedian, actor, writer, singer, and director. He is the lead actor and star in the Beverly Hills Cop film franchise , in which he portrays the now-iconic role of Axel Foley . Overview [ ] Eddie Murphy has become a world renown actor and comedian for his iconic portrayals of characters throughout his decades-long career. His breakout role came in the 1984 film, Beverly Hills Cop as Axel Foley . He reprised his role in three additional sequels, including the newest film that takes place 40 years after the first film. Murphy is credited as a story writer for Beverly Hills Cop II . Murphy went on to star in dozens of films, including Shrek , Dr. Doolittle , Mulan , and Coming to America . He is widely praised as one of the best comedians of all time.
